Bitcoin ETFs see $500M inflows as institutional adoption grows

By CoinDesk • Today, 14:30 GMT+7 • 5 min read

Major institutional players are ramping up their cryptocurrency investments, driving record inflows into newly approved spot Bitcoin ETFs this week. The total net inflows have surpassed $500 million, signaling strong market confidence despite recent price volatility.

Analysts suggest that the rapid accumulation by large asset managers could lead to a supply squeeze in the coming months, especially as the highly anticipated Bitcoin halving event approaches.

"We are witnessing an unprecedented shift in capital allocation. Traditional finance is no longer just observing; they are actively participating," said a senior analyst at a prominent investment firm. "The ETF wrapper has removed significant barriers to entry for institutional capital."

Market sentiment remains highly bullish, with Bitcoin testing crucial resistance levels at the $65,000 mark. If the momentum sustains, many technical indicators point towards new all-time highs before the end of the quarter.
